# byte-farm
a CLI farming simulator!

## The Game
Plant and grow plants! List plants on the market for profit (or for loss)! Find enthusiastic people to join your cult to greater influence the market! Summon Eldritch deities and discover plants from otherworlds to become the god of farming! The possibilities are quite large...

## Q&A (自问自答)
Q. Why would anyone play this?  
A. To find eternal happiness as a cult leader. And to farm plants on your terminal emulator.

Q. Who would you recommend this game to?
